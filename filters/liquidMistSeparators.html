LIQUID & MIST REMOVAL - FOR VACUUM SYSTEMS

Solberg offers various solutions for removing potentially harmful liquids, sludge, and particulates and keeping these contaminants from reaching vacuum pump inlets. Our multistage liquid separators and inlet vacuum filters complete your vacuum pump systems and can be configured to meet your unique needs. Our compact liquid separator features a clear view trap that makes visual inspections simple, and our liquid knockout tanks are designed to be easy to install and maintain.
